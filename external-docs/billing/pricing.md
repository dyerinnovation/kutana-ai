# Pricing & Plans

Kutana offers four subscription tiers. All plans include live transcription, automatic task extraction, and browser-based meeting access.

## Plans

| Feature | Basic | Pro | Business | Enterprise |
|---------|-------|-----|----------|------------|
| **Price** | $7.99/mo | $29/mo | $79/mo | $150/mo |
| **Yearly** | $79/yr | $290/yr | $790/yr | Custom |
| Meetings per month | 10 | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Custom agent configs | 1 | 5 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Feed integrations | 0 | 2 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Managed agents | - | - | Included | Unlimited |
| API key access | - | - | Full | Full |
| TTS | - | Kutana TTS | Premium TTS | Premium TTS |

## Billing management

Manage your subscription from **Settings > Billing** in the Kutana dashboard. From there you can:

- View your current plan and usage
- Upgrade or downgrade your plan
- Update payment methods
- View invoice history

Billing is handled securely through Stripe. Kutana never stores your payment details directly.

## Upgrade prompts

When you reach a plan limit (e.g., maximum number of agents or feeds), the UI shows an upgrade badge linking to the pricing page. The backend enforces limits with `402` (payment required) or `403` (tier too low) responses.

## Trial

New accounts start on a 14-day trial with Basic-tier access. A credit card is required at signup. If you don't cancel before the trial ends, billing starts automatically.
