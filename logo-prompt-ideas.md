# Kutana AI — Logo / Icon Prompt Ideas

Five detailed prompts for NanaBanana (or any AI image generator).
Each describes a distinct visual direction. All outputs should be **1024×1024 px**, square format, suitable for use as an app icon.

---

## Prompt 1 — C-Ring with Convergence Nodes *(recommended)*

> A minimal, geometric app icon on a very dark near-black background with a subtle violet undertone. The icon is a partial circle (a "C" shape, open on the right) rendered as a clean arc stroke in white. Three small glowing white dots rest on the arc — one at the top, one at the far left, one at the bottom — representing meeting participants arranged in a circle. A fourth, slightly brighter dot sits in the gap on the right side of the arc, representing the AI agent connecting to the meeting. The arc and dots have a soft violet-indigo inner glow. The overall color palette is deep space black (#07070E) background, pure white strokes, and accents of violet (#8457F6). Flat 2D vector style, no gradients on the marks themselves, sharp and precise — in the spirit of Linear or Raycast's icon language.

---

## Prompt 2 — Gradient Crystal Mark

> A modern app icon: a geometric crystal or gemstone shape in 2D, rendered as a stylized hexagonal or rhombus-like faceted mark. The facets are filled with smooth gradient tones that shift from pale violet-lavender (#C4B5FD) at the top to deep indigo-purple (#4C1D95) at the bottom, with a thin bright highlight edge on one facet catching virtual light. Set on a rich near-black background (#07070E). No text. The crystal subtly evokes both clarity of thought and intelligence — appropriate for an AI platform. The overall silhouette is tight, symmetrical, and would read clearly at 16×16px. Style: flat illustration with a slight 2.5D depth illusion. Reference aesthetic: Notion, Obsidian, Arc Browser.

---

## Prompt 3 — Neural Orbit / Atom

> An abstract app icon depicting a luminous particle or nucleus at center, surrounded by two or three thin elliptical orbital rings at slightly different angles — like electrons orbiting an atom, or agents orbiting a central meeting. The rings are razor-thin and glow with a soft violet-to-cyan gradient. The central nucleus is a small, bright circle emitting a soft violet radial bloom. Background: very dark cool black (#060610). No text. The composition reads as both scientific precision and futuristic AI intelligence. Render style: clean flat-ish illustration with subtle glow bloom FX, no heavy 3D shading. Color palette: deep space black, violet (#7C3AED), electric indigo (#4F46E5), faint cyan accent (#22D3EE). Think Vercel meets CERN.

---

## Prompt 4 — Converging Waveform

> A horizontal audio waveform icon that morphs and converges toward the center, suggesting active listening and real-time speech processing. The waveform bars are symmetric, tallest in the middle, tapering to shorter bars on the edges — but instead of a static waveform, the outer bars curve inward as if being "pulled" toward the center point, creating a lens or focus metaphor. Colors: bars start as muted gray-violet on the edges and intensify to bright violet-white (#9F7AEA → white) at the center peak. Background: very dark near-black (#07070E). The icon communicates AI listening, voice, and meeting intelligence. Style: clean SVG-like precision, no textures, minimal and high-contrast. Similar aesthetic feel to the Whisper or ElevenLabs brand marks.

---

## Prompt 5 — Abstract Letterform "C" with AI Pulse

> A bold, stylized letter "C" as an app icon. The letter is rendered not as a traditional typeface glyph but as two concentric arcs with a visible gap between them — a thick outer arc and a thinner inner arc — creating a ring-like depth effect. Inside the gap, a small pulsing dot (like a cursor or a single neuron firing) sits at the open end. The letter has a smooth violet-to-indigo gradient fill (#9E7AFF to #4E18B0) and a very subtle outer glow. Background: transparent or near-black (#07070E). The result feels like a premium monogram mark for a developer tool — restrained, purposeful, and immediately memorable at any size. Aesthetic references: Vercel triangle, Linear L-mark, GitHub Octocat in terms of simplicity and precision.

---

## Design Notes for All Prompts

- **Primary brand color**: Violet `#6D32ED` / `#8457F6` (mid-weight) / `#9E7AFF` (highlight)
- **Background**: Very dark near-black with cool violet undertone `#07070E`
- **Style**: Flat to near-flat. No heavy drop shadows or skeuomorphic textures.
- **Size**: Must read clearly at 16×16, 32×32, 64×64, and 1024×1024.
- **No text** in the icon — pure mark only.
- **Output format**: PNG with transparent background preferred for versatility.
