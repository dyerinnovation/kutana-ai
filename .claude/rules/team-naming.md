# Team Naming Convention

Name agent teams `YYYY-MM-DD-dev-N` (e.g., `2026-04-12-dev-1`). Teams are persistent and reused across phases within a session. Teammates take on different roles (devs, testers, deployers, doc-writers, bookkeepers) as needed.

Do not name teams after specific tasks (e.g., `livekit-phase0`). Only create a new team when the previous one's session is stale or dead.
